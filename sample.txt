Nepal is a landlocked country in South Asia, situated between India and China. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, including the Himalayas and Mount Everest, the world's highest peak. The capital city, Kathmandu, is rich in history, culture, and ancient temples. Nepal is home to diverse ethnic groups, languages, and traditions, making it a culturally vibrant nation. Tourism, agriculture, and remittances play important roles in the country's economy, while its warm hospitality attracts visitors from around the world.
